IC-9 "Winand" (Ice Crane-9, also know as "Glory", industrial designation: Object-724) is a super heavy tank in the Chinese novel ‘the Moon War'

The tank has 4 tracks (2 pairs). It’s 150~170 Tons heavy. Front turret has a 152mm/25L Chinese-Soviet Gun. Main (Middle) Turret has a British 183 mm L4 Ship gun.
