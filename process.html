This model can separate into 4 parts, as you can see: Front Turret, Main/Middle Turret, Body and Suspension System.
One of the hardest part is front turret, because it's base on a half ball. I extrude for 2 hours and I get a half of turret like this. To make it symmetry，I copied and mirrored another one, and join them together.
For all the gun barrel and muzzle brake, I joined two tubes (not cylinder, with no top and bottom); I used cylinder in observe Turret (little Cylinders on the top of turrets) as well.
Another hard part is suspension system, there are many cylinders and rings in it.
